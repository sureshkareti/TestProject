Additional requirements for USA:

a: If the supplier is U.S.A. based and doing business with Agilent Technologies, Inc. USA, the supplier is required to submit W9 Form and also need to provide Tax Info under ‘1099-1042’ tab of Supplier Addition Request Form.

b: If the supplier is not U.S.A. based but doing business with Agilent Technologies, Inc. USA, the supplier is required to submit W8 Form and also need to provide Tax Info under ‘1099-1042’ Supplier Addition Request Form.
